How It Began

Rooted in a Love for Lasting Beauty

Eternal Flowers was born in 2018 from a simple yet profound realization: the most beautiful moments deserve to be preserved. Our founder, Elena Rousseau, a third-generation florist from the South of France, grew up surrounded by the ephemeral beauty of fresh-cut flowers. She dreamed of creating arrangements that would not wilt after a week but would continue to inspire for years.

After extensive research into preservation techniques and partnerships with the finest rose growers in Ecuador and the Netherlands, Elena developed a proprietary method that maintains each flower's natural softness, color vibrancy, and delicate structure for up to three years.

Today, Eternal Flowers is recognized among the world's premier preserved floral ateliers, with a flagship boutique on Fifth Avenue and a clientele that spans the globe.

Our Mission

To transform the way the world experiences floral luxury by creating preserved arrangements of unparalleled quality—pieces that honor nature's artistry while offering enduring elegance.

Our Vision

To become the definitive name in preserved floristry worldwide, setting new standards for craftsmanship, sustainability, and timeless design that transcends generations.

Our Promise
